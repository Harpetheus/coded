Living for webdesign

Hey there! I'm Brett, a passionate and innovative web designer with an insatiable curiosity for all things design and technology. With a background in fine arts and a full stack web development degree from BCIT, I have honed my skills to craft visually stunning and user-friendly websites that leave a lasting impact on visitors.

My Journey into Web Design

Ever since I laid my eyes on the mesmerizing world of the Internet, I knew I had found my calling. It all began with tinkering around simple HTML and CSS codes, curiously experimenting with layouts, and creating my first website, which was admittedly a bit rough around the edges, but it ignited a flame inside me. That moment marked the beginning of my journey into the ever-evolving realm of web design.

My Design Process

Collaboration lies at the core of my design process. I firmly believe in open communication with my clients to understand their aspirations, target audience, and unique selling points. By empathizing with their goals, I can transform their ideas into visually captivating realities.
